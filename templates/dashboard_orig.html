Sort debtors (ascending debt) and creditors (descending credit) using Merge Sort — O(n log n).

Greedy Selection

Pick max debtor and max creditor. Settle min(|debt|, credit) between them.

4
Update & Repeat

Subtract settled amount from both. If one reaches zero, advance that pointer. Repeat until all settled.
